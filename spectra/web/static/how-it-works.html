How Spectra Works

Spectra reads a forward-facing driving video frame by frame, builds a picture of the road and the traffic in it, and asks one question for every object: is this on a collision course with my lane? The map below is the full pipeline. Open any stage or the topic menu for the details.

Per-Object Risk Evaluation

Solid Internal risk step Brighter Used downstream

This page covers the risk decision path: the three TTC cues and their fusion, the four weighted signal terms, the trust and lane gates, the final risk score, and the banding into SAFE / CAUTION / DANGER.

Time-to-collision
ttc_depth = distance / closing_mps
ttc_expansion = 1 / expansion_rate
ttc_flow = 1 / radial_expansion(flow, p75)
fused_ttc = weighted_median(ttc_depth, ttc_flow, ttc_expansion)
eta_pressure(ttc) = clamp((3 − ttc) / 3, 0, 1)
approach = 0.50 × closing_mps + 0.30 × expansion + 0.20 × flow
Risk score
signal = 0.40 × eta_pressure + 0.30 × proximity + 0.25 × approach + 0.05 × brake
gate = 0.65 + 0.35 × confidence
relevance = crossing_risk
risk.score = gate × relevance × signal
State banding
state = DANGER if risk.score ≥ 0.60
state = CAUTION if risk.score ≥ 0.25
state = SAFE otherwise
escalate → DANGER if fused_ttc < 1.0s (≥2 frames) and state ≥ CAUTION
force → SAFE if detection_confidence < 0.20
stabilize: upgrade after 3 frames, downgrade after 7 frames

TTC Cues

Depth / Kalman Distance

Smooths object distance and closing speed from metric depth (the physical backbone).

BBox Expansion

Measures object scale growth across track history as a visual approach cue.

Flow TTC

Estimates time-to-collision from radial optical flow around the vanishing point.

TTC Fusion

Blends the depth, flow and expansion cues with a confidence-weighted median and agreement scoring.

Collision ETA

Uses depth distance and closing speed for the physical ETA shown in the UI.

Signal Terms

ETA Pressure

Scores how little collision time remains from the fused TTC.

Proximity

Scores how close the object already is from metric depth.

Approach

Scores how strongly distance is closing from closing speed, expansion and flow.

Brake Light Cue

Adds a small early warning from lead-vehicle brake lamps.

Gates + Score

Confidence Smoothing

Stabilizes per-track confidence used by the trust gate and display.

Confidence Gate

Scales the score so low-trust detections cannot dominate the result.

Lane Relevance

Gates the entire score by the probability the object is in the ego lane at impact.

Risk Score

Produces the continuous object risk score from 0 to 1.
