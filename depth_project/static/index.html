How Zone Flow Works

Processing Pipeline

01
Loading & Pre-processing

Frames are loaded and normalized for velocity analysis.

video_loader.py preprocess.py
02
Optical Flow

Calculates dense pixel-level motion vectors using Farneback's algorithm.

optical_flow.py
03
Depth Estimation

A neural estimator predicts the nearness (inverse depth) of every pixel.

depth_estimator.py
04
Risk Fusion & TTC

Combines motion and depth to calculate Time-to-Collision (TTC).

fusion.py risk_calculator.py
05
Annotation & UI

Results are visualized and sent to the dashboard via API.

annotator.py api.py

Logic Details

Zone Mapping

When autonomous object detection (YOLO) is disabled, the system segments the field of view into three distinct monitoring zones:

Left0 - 1/3
Center1/3 - 2/3
Right2/3 - 1

Spatial risk is analyzed by aggregating depth and motion vectors within these coordinate boundaries.

Risk Scoring

A weighted fusion algorithm determines the overall hazard level by combining spatial and temporal metrics:

Hazard = 0.6 Nearness + 0.4 Speed
Nearness: Depth proximity Speed: Closing velocity
Alert Thresholds

System triggers critical events based on the predicted Time-to-Collision (TTC) window:

DANGER < 1.5s
CAUTION 1.5s - 3.0s
SAFE > 3.0s
